01 — Auth

TikTok OAuth 2.0

Secure login via TikTok's official OAuth flow. Only the minimum required scopes are requested: user.info.basic, video.publish, video.list.

02 — Publish

Auto Scheduler

Upload your video, pick a date and time, and TokAuto handles the rest. The scheduler polls TikTok's API every minute and publishes at the exact planned time.

03 — Privacy

Personal Use Only

TokAuto is a single-user tool. No data is collected, sold, or shared. Your TikTok credentials are stored only in your own session and never transmitted to third parties.
